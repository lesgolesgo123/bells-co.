Delivery and Returns

Delivery Information

We offer several shipping options to meet your needs. All orders are processed within 1-2 business days.

Service Delivery Time Cost
Standard Shipping 3-5 business days RM10 (Free for orders over RM200)
Express Shipping 1-2 business days RM20
Same Day Delivery (Kuala Lumpur) Same day (Order before 12pm) RM30

Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Our return policy includes:

  • 30-day return period from delivery date
  • Items must be unworn, unwashed, and with original tags
  • Original packaging should be included
  • Free returns for orders over RM200
  • Refunds processed within 5-7 business days

How to Return

  1. Log into your account and navigate to "My Orders"
  2. Select the item(s) you wish to return
  3. Print the return label provided
  4. Package the items securely with the return label
  5. Drop off at any authorized courier location

International Shipping

We ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days. Additional customs duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer.
