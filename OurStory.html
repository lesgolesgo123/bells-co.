OUR STORY

More Than Just Clothing

Our Beginning

Bells & Co. began not as a business venture, but as a heartfelt mission. Founded in 2020 by Dr. Javid Iqbal, our company was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to provide high-quality clothing while making a meaningful difference in the lives of children in need.

What started as a small collection of carefully crafted sweaters has grown into a beloved brand known for its commitment to quality, style, and ethics. Today, we continue to honor our founder's original vision with every piece we create.

Dr. Javid Iqbal
Founder & Visionary

Alongside Dr. Javid Iqbal's initial vision, Bells & Co. has remained true to its core mission while expanding our collections to include clothing for the whole family.

25%

25% of all Bells & Co. profits will be donated to children's charities worldwide. This commitment is at the core of everything we do.

Every purchase you make directly contributes to providing education, healthcare, and basic necessities for children in underpriviledged communities. Together, we've helped thousands of children build brighter futures.
