More Than Just Clothing

Our Beginning

Bells & Co. began not as a business venture, but as a heartfelt mission. Founded in 2015 by Dr. Javid Iqbal, our company was born from a simple yet powerful vision: to provide high-quality, beautiful clothing while making a meaningful difference in the lives of children in need.

What started as a small collection of carefully crafted sweaters has grown into a beloved brand known for its commitment to quality, style, and social responsibility. Today, we continue to honor our founder's original vision with every piece we create.

Dr. Javid Iqbal
Founder & Visionary

A humanitarian at heart, Dr. Javid Iqbal has dedicated his life to making a positive impact. With a background in medicine and a passion for social justice, he envisioned a clothing brand that could do more than just sell garments‚Äîit could change lives.

25%

From the goodness of his heart, Dr. Javid Iqbal established that 25% of all Bells & Co. profits would be donated to children's charities worldwide. This commitment is at the core of everything we do.

Every purchase you make directly contributes to providing education, healthcare, and basic necessities for children in underserved communities. Together, we've helped thousands of children build brighter futures.
