Frequently Asked Questions

WHAT IS YOUR RETURN POLICY?
We offer a 30-day return policy for all unworn items with original tags attached. Returns are free for orders over RM200. Simply log into your account, generate a return label, and drop off your package at any authorized courier location.
HOW LONG DOES SHIPPING TAKE?
Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within Malaysia. Express shipping (RM20) delivers within 1-2 business days. We also offer same-day delivery in Kuala Lumpur for orders placed before 12pm (RM30). International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days.
DO YOU SHIP INTERNATIONALLY?
Yes, we ship to over 50 countries worldwide. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days. Please note that additional customs duties and taxes may apply and are the responsibility of the customer. You can check shipping rates at checkout.
HOW DO I TRACK MY ORDER?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track your order by logging into your account and navigating to "My Orders." Click on the order number to view real-time tracking information from our delivery partners.
WHAT PAYMENT METHODS DO YOU ACCEPT?
We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), online banking transfers, and e-wallets (Touch 'n Go, GrabPay, Boost). All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway. Your payment information is never stored on our servers.
HOW DO I CARE FOR MY BELLS & CO. GARMENTS?
Check the care label on each garment. Most items require gentle cold wash and air drying for best results. For wool sweaters, we recommend hand washing in cold water and laying flat to dry. Avoid using bleach or fabric softeners, as they can damage the fibers and colors.
WHAT IS YOUR SIZE CHART?
We provide detailed size charts for each product category (Women, Men, Kids). You can find the size chart on each product page. We recommend measuring yourself and comparing with our charts for the best fit. If you're between sizes, we suggest sizing up for a more comfortable fit.
DO YOU OFFER GIFT WRAPPING?
Yes! We offer complimentary gift wrapping during the holiday season (November-December). For other times of the year, gift wrapping is available for RM15 per item. You can select gift wrapping at checkout and include a personalized message for the recipient.
HOW CAN I CONTACT CUSTOMER SERVICE?
You can reach our customer service team via email at support@bellsco.com or by phone at +6012-345-6789. Our team is available Monday to Friday, 9am-6pm (Malaysia time). For urgent inquiries, we recommend using our live chat feature available on the website during business hours.
WHAT IS YOUR CHARITY INITIATIVE?
Following our founder Dr. Javid Iqbal's vision, we donate 25% of all profits to children's charities worldwide. This commitment helps provide education, healthcare, and basic necessities for children in underserved communities. Every purchase you make directly contributes to this cause.
